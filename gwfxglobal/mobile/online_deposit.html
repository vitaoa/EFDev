NOTICES FOR DEPOSIT
  1. All cleared funds will be immediately reflected in your account of the trading system, which serves in lieu of any credit advice.

  2. To comply with Anti-money Laundering Policy, please note that third party deposit accepted is not accepted in any circumstances.

  3. The minimum amount for each single deposit is US$50.

  4. The designated account currency is U.S. dollars.

  5. Deposits in currencies other than US dollar will be converted into account currency according to the market exchange rates of third-party online payment gateway. The amount of money after the conversion may be different from the original amount. The exchange rate is based on the exchange price of the third-party online payment gateway which will be updated from time to time.

  6. The Company shall perform due diligence on the review procedures as required by the regulatory authorities from time to time. If customers fail to submit deposit proofs or if their submitted bank account information is not complete, it may result in delays in fund transfer.
