Notices for Withdrawal
  1. The daily fund withdrawal handling time is from GMT+8 09:00 – 16:00.

  2. The exchange rates shown in the trading platform is for reference only. The actual rates are subject to the banks.

  3. The credit amount shown is for reference only (deductions due to handling fees may be incurred).

  4. To prevent any delays, please ensure the bank account information you submitted is correct and complete before submitting withdrawal request. The withdrawal amount will be debited to your registered bank account and its currency will be same as the one of the bank account.

  5. A handling fee of US20 will be charged for any single withdrawal less than US$80.

  6. A handling fee of 6% of the withdrawal amount will be charged if customers’ trading volume is less than 50% of their deposit amount. The handling fee will be waived except the circumstance mentioned.

  7. The maximum frequency of withdrawals is 3 times per day. If the frequency of withdrawal is over the limit, it may result in delays in fund transfer.

  8. Customers can withdraw the balance in their account at the weekends only if the the weekend margin is maintained as 100% of the initial margin.

  9. Customers need to bear any handling fees incurred if the withdrawal amount is needed to be transferred by telegraphic transfer (T/T).
