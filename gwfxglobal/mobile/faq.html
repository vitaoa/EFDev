  • Q : What is Rollover? A : Rollover is the interest paid or earned for holding a position overnight. Every trade involves not only two different currencies, but also their two different interest rates. If the interest rate on the currency you bought is higher than the interest rate of the currency you sold, then you will receive rollover interest. If the interest rate on the currency you bought is lower than the interest rate on the currency you sold, then you will need to pay rollover interest.
